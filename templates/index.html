This is not a bet game

Soccer Master does not have an odds system. In general you will win a fixed amount of fictional money.

Guessing system

If you guess right the result of a game, you will win $100.00. Otherwise, guessing wrong will cost you $30.00

Combined feature

You can select more than one game and make a combined guess. If you get all the results right, you will win what you normally would multiplied by the number of games you guessed. On the other hand, if you get just one game wrong, you will lose $30.00 multiplied by the number of games.

Level

At the beggining, you are only be able to guess 5 games, until the 5 are finished. But by increasing your level (by getting more and more results right), you will be able to guess more games.
